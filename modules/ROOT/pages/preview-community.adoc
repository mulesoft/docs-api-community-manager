After you edit your Salesforce Experience Cloud site, you can preview the new version to ensure that it meets your expectations:

. Open the Anypoint API Community Manager control panel.
. Click *Community Builder*.
. Click *Preview*.
. Verify that your changes are applied and display correctly. APIs that are visible only to administrators appear in this preview.
. Click *Back to Builder*.
